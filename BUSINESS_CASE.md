🚨 Business Problem

The organization currently lacks clear insights into:

❓ Which customers are most likely to churn
💰 The financial impact of customer attrition
🔍 The key behavioral and contractual drivers of churn
🎯 Which customer segments should be prioritized for retention

📉 Without this visibility, the company risks:

Losing high-value customers
Misallocating resources in retention efforts
🎯 Objectives

This project aims to:

📊 Measure and monitor overall churn rate
💰 Quantify total revenue at risk
🔎 Identify key drivers of customer attrition
👥 Segment customers based on risk and value
🧠 Provide actionable insights to support retention strategies
👥 Stakeholders
🧑‍💼 Executive Leadership (CEO, CFO)
📢 Marketing & Customer Retention Teams
🛠️ Product & Service Strategy Teams
📏 Success Metrics

The project will be considered successful if it enables stakeholders to:

⚡ Quickly assess churn rate and revenue exposure
🎯 Identify high-risk customer segments
🔍 Understand key drivers of churn behavior
📌 Prioritize data-driven retention strategies
🧠 Make informed decisions within minutes, not hours
🧑‍💻 My Role

As the Data Analyst, I am responsible for:

🔄 Preparing and transforming raw customer data
🏗️ Designing a structured data model
📊 Defining key business metrics (KPIs)
📈 Building executive-level dashboards
💡 Translating data into actionable business insights
🚀 Expected Business Impact

This solution will enable the organization to:

📉 Reduce customer churn through targeted interventions
💰 Protect recurring revenue streams
🎯 Improve retention strategies
🔍 Gain a clearer understanding of customer behavior
✨ Final Note

This business case highlights how data-driven insights can transform churn analysis into a strategic revenue protection tool, empowering leadership to act proactively rather than reactively.
